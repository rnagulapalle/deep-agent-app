# Who Is the Author of the Ava and Maya Fairy Dragon Books?

## Overview

The books featuring **Ava and Maya as fairy dragon characters** are part of the **Dragon Girls** series, written by **Maddy Mara**—a pen name for two Australian authors collaborating: **Hilary Rogers** and **Meredith Badger**. The series is published by **Scholastic** and targets early readers ages 6-9.

The fairy dragons (including Ava and Maya) represent one of several themed sub-series within the broader Dragon Girls collection, which also includes Glitter Dragons, Treasure Dragons, Shadow Dragons, and Sea Dragons.
