# Who Is the Author of the Ava and Maya Fairy Dragon Books: A Pragmatist's Guide

## Overview

The **Dragon Girls** book series, which features fairy dragon characters including Ava and Maya, is written by **Maddy Mara**. This is a pen name used by two Australian authors working collaboratively: **Hilary Rogers** and **Meredith Badger**.

---

## Key Facts About the Author(s)

### The Writing Team Behind "Maddy Mara"

| Author | Background |
|--------|------------|
| **Hilary Rogers** | Australian children's book author and editor |
| **Meredith Badger** | Prolific Australian author of children's and middle-grade fiction |

- **Pen Name Purpose**: Using "Maddy Mara" allows the duo to brand their collaborative work distinctly
- **Publisher**: The series is published by **Scholastic**, a major children's book publisher
- **Target Audience**: Early readers, typically ages 6-9

## The Dragon Girls Series: Practical Information

### Series Structure

The Dragon Girls series is organized into themed sub-series featuring different types of dragons:

- **Glitter Dragons** (including characters like Azmina, Willa, Naomi)
- **Treasure Dragons**
- **Fairy Dragons** (where Ava and Maya appear)
- **Shadow Dragons**
- **Sea Dragons**
